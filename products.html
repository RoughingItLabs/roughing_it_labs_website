AutoMind

Featured

AI-Powered OBD-II Diagnostic & Vehicle Health Companion

AutoMind transforms your vehicle diagnostics experience by using advanced artificial intelligence and OBD-II technology to provide intelligent, real-time vehicle health monitoring and personalized maintenance recommendations based on your driving patterns, vehicle history, and diagnostic data.

Key Features:

  • Smart Diagnostic Scanning: Connect to any OBD-II port and get instant AI analysis of your vehicle's health
  • AI Trouble Code Analysis: Intelligent interpretation of DTCs with actionable repair recommendations
  • Intelligent PID Monitoring: AI recommends optimal parameters to monitor based on your specific vehicle
  • Predictive Maintenance: Anticipates potential issues before they become costly repairs
  • AI Chat Assistant: Get instant answers about your vehicle's condition and maintenance needs
  • VIN Intelligence: Automatic vehicle identification and model-specific diagnostic insights
  • Real-Time Data Explorer: Live monitoring of engine performance with AI-powered analysis
