DinerDroid is Now Live on Google Play Store!

August 4, 2025 RoughingIt Labs Team Launch News 3 min read

DinerDroid is now available for download on the Google Play Store!

After months of development, testing, and fine-tuning, we're thrilled to announce that DinerDroid is officially available on the Google Play Store!

What is DinerDroid?

DinerDroid is the first AI that truly understands how you talk about food. Say "I love steak!" or "I don't like mushrooms!" and our AI remembers. Then, when you scan any menu, DinerDroid finds perfect dishes based on your natural language preferences, dietary needs, and even your current mood.

Revolutionary Features

  • Natural Language Preferences: Tell us "I'm obsessed with spicy Thai food" and we remember forever
  • Five Preference Categories: Food, beverages, desserts, dietary restrictions, and allergies
  • Real-Time Context: Add current mood like "I need comfort food today" right on the main screen
  • AI Explanations: "This was recommended because you love steak and grilled proteins"
  • Learning from History: Thumbs up/down dishes you've tried for continuous improvement

Built by Food Lovers, for Food Lovers

As part of RoughingIt Labs' mission to pioneer AI solutions, DinerDroid represents our passion for combining cutting-edge technology with everyday dining decisions. We believe that choosing what to eat should be confident and exciting – not stressful or overwhelming.
