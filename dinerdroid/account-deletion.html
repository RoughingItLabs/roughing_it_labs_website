Account Deletion

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, preferences, dining history, and purchased tokens will be permanently deleted.
Account Deletion via Mobile App
To delete your DinerDroid account, please follow these steps in the mobile app:
1 Open the DinerDroid app on your mobile device
2 Go to Settings - Tap the menu or profile icon and select "Settings"
3 Scroll down to Account Management section
4 Tap "Delete Account" - This is located in the Account Management section
5 Confirm deletion - Read the warning and confirm your decision
6 Wait for completion - The app will delete all your data and sign you out
What gets deleted:
• Your user account and profile
• All dining history and preferences
• Saved settings and configurations
• Token balance and purchase history
• All personal data stored by DinerDroid

Need Help?

If you're unable to delete your account through the app or have any questions, please contact our support team:

support@roughingitlabs.com

Please include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line and provide your registered email address.
